Feedback

What feedback should I give?

Any amount of feedback is appreciated! If you are reporting any errors, please be specific about how you encountered that error.


If you want to give feedback but do not know where to start, here are a few topics you may comment on:

  • The website's user interface
  • The website's overall user experience
  • Features you want to see implemented in the future
  • How helpful was this website?
  • Was this website interesting?
  • Potential improvements on any part of the website

Where can I write feedback?

You can provide your feedback by filling out this anonymous Google Form!
